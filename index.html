Main Goal
An enthusiastic student takes courses that are both trackable and sharable. The website keeps track of their progress until the student has completed the course. Once the student has taken a full course, they can share the great news on different social media outlets and with employers and clients.

Steps
  1. The student logs onto the website, so their studentId (profile, account) can be credited for the course when done.
  2. The student then watches the courses.
  3. The student can take notes and read the transcript on the system.
  4. Once the student finishes watching a course, the system shows they have completed the course.
  5. The student then can share their knowledge gain through social media outlets.
