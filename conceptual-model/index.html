Model for Lynda.com

There are actually four personas for Lynda.com, they are as follows:

  1. Schools
      The education segment has had the highest adoption rate and uses lynda.com for training, students, faculty and staff.
  2. Corporate Enterprise
  3. Government
  4. Individuals

Schools

Corporate Enterprise

The corporate enterprise segment views lynda.com as a mixture of a help desk substitute and a just-in-time training partner, and a lot of the subject matter they need to teach is baseline business skills.

Government

The government segment uses lynda.com mainly for staff training and continuing education.

Individuals

Individuals use Lynda.com to further their own personal education and professional growth.
